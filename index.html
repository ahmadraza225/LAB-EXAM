A SOCIETAL MISSION

The mission of Air University is to achieve excellence in teaching and research for producing graduates
with sound professional knowledge, integrity of character, a keen sense of social responsibility and a
passion for lifelong learning. The University shall stand committed to creating an environment conducive
for attracting the best students, faculty and supporting staff for contributing to the development of a
prosperous, peaceful and enlightened society.
